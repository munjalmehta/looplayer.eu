The Textile Recycling MarketplaceDer Textilrecycling-Marktplatz

The foundation. Manufacturers and municipalities list textile waste lots. Verified, GRS-certified recyclers submit bids. Deals close via DocuSign, settle via Stripe. At delivery confirmation, Layers 2 and 3 fire automatically. No other compliance vendor runs this — they all work upstream. LoopLayer works where the material actually moves.Das Fundament. Bei Lieferbestätigung lösen Schichten 2 und 3 automatisch aus.
